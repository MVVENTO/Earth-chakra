Root Chakra (Muladhara): Located at Mount Shasta, California, USA, or at the base of the spine.
Sacral Chakra (Svadhisthana): Associated with Lake Titicaca, Peru/Bolivia, or with specific underwater locations.
Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura): Often linked with Uluru (Ayers Rock), Australia, or with other significant rock formations.
Heart Chakra (Anahata): Associated with Glastonbury and Shaftesbury, England, or with other sacred sites.
Throat Chakra (Vishuddha): Often connected with the Great Pyramid of Giza, Egypt, or with other pyramids and temples.
Third Eye Chakra (Ajna): Linked with Mount Kailash, Tibet, or with other high mountain peaks.
Crown Chakra (Sahasrara): Associated with Mount Everest, Nepal/Tibet, or with the North or South Pole.
